What I Believe





I believe movement is life
I believe you can heal your body with a return to simple functional movement patterns
I believe it is never too late to create change in a body and it can happen faster than you ever thought possible.
I believe your body know what it needs
I believe every system in your body relies on a functional musculoskeletal system. The musculoskeletal system is the body system that provides the framework for movement and stability.

All systems in the body work on movement:
• the heart beats
• the lungs breathe
• the stomach grinds
• the colon transports the blood flows
• the nerves conduct
• the muscles contract

Pain in the body begins when movement patterns are impaired.
Restore your bodies proper design for movement and the pain will cease.
No fancy machinery or pills required; your body already has everything it needs to heal.

I believe you need to trust yourself; you have the ability to help yourself.
I believe you need to take responsibility for your present state of health.
I believe with your commitment to change, you can become free of pain.
I believe your age does not limit you from feeling good again.
It is never too late to begin. Let's get started.
